Undergraduates

If you are interested in a fourth year honours project (BIOL 479/480) apply directly to Dr. David Dankort ASAP. We are always looking for new undergrads!

Graduate Students

We are looking for students interested in research at the PhD/MSc level. Students with external scholarships or awards will be prioritized but this is not an absolute requirement. If you are interested in applying please contact Dr. David Dankort or if you would like to know a little bit more about the lab feel free to email any one of our lab members with your questions!

Postdoctoral Fellows

Experience with genetically modified mice is required. Apply with CV and statement of interest to Dr. David Dankort.
